--- category: - Current - E-Health creator: jnicholson thumbnail: /img/uploads/r-ONLINE-ACCESS-HEALTH-INFORMATION-large570.jpg is_sticky: 0 link: >- https://pactlab.co.uk/portfolio/going-online-for-health-noting-changes-over-time/ slug: going-online-for-health-noting-changes-over-time title: 'Going online for health: Noting changes over time' post_date_gmt: '2017-06-05 09:33:47' pub_date: Mon, 05 Jun 2017 09:33:47 +0000 --- As part of an ongoing project PactLab researchers have used survey methodology to take a snapshot of e-health users every five years since the year 2000 in order to assess changing patterns of use and engagement with online health information and advice. A large scale questionnaire has allowed us to note changes in website use and to model the factors that predict trust in online health advice across a range of different user groups.
