The project is a collaboration between Newcastle University and Northumbria University. In particular, it involves researchers from Northumbria University’s Psychology and Communication Technology (PaCT) Lab and Warning, Advice & Reporting Point (nuWARP) and Newcastle University’s School of Computing Science and Centre for Cybercrime and Computer Security (CCCS). For more information about the ChAISE project, please select one of the following two links: http://www.ucl.ac.uk/cybersecurity/Projects/choice_architecture http://research.ncl.ac.uk/choicearchitecture/
